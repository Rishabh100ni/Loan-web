What is a Credit Score?

A CIBIL score between 300–900 reflects your creditworthiness. Higher scores unlock better rates and faster approvals.

How to Improve

6 Proven Tips to Boost Your Score

01

Pay EMIs On Time

Payment history is the biggest factor. Set auto-debit to never miss a due date.

02

Keep Credit Utilisation Under 30%

Using too much of your credit card limit hurts your score. Keep usage low.

03

Don't Apply for Multiple Loans

Multiple hard enquiries in short time reduce your score. Apply only when needed.

04

Maintain Old Accounts

Older credit accounts increase your credit history length, boosting your score.

05

Diversify Your Credit Mix

Having a mix of secured and unsecured credit shows responsible borrowing.

06

Check Your Report Regularly

Errors in your report can drag your score. Dispute them via CIBIL's portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about OctoWealth loans.

General

OctoWealth is a registered loan facilitation company based in Banswara, Rajasthan. We connect borrowers with 20+ leading banks to offer personal, business, home, education, vehicle, and gold loans at competitive rates.
OctoWealth operates as a loan distributor/DSA (Direct Sales Agent) authorized by our partner banks. All loans are sanctioned and disbursed directly by RBI-registered banks and NBFCs.

Eligibility

You must be at least 21 years old at the time of application and not more than 65 years at loan maturity.
A minimum CIBIL score of 650 is required. Scores of 750+ receive the best interest rates and fastest approvals.

Repayment

Yes. Prepayment is allowed at any point during the loan tenure. There are zero foreclosure charges after 12 months of disbursement.
A late payment fee of 2% per month is charged on overdue amount, and it may impact your credit score. Please contact us immediately if you anticipate payment difficulty.

Security

Yes. We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission and comply with RBI data security guidelines and India's PDPB Act.
Your data is shared only with our partner banks for loan processing purposes, with your explicit consent. We do not sell data to any third party.

OctoWealth collects personal and financial information solely for the purpose of loan processing and customer service. Your data is encrypted, never sold, and shared only with authorized banking partners with your consent. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Terms & Conditions

By using OctoWealth's services, you agree to our terms. OctoWealth acts as a facilitator between borrowers and lenders. Loan approval, rates, and disbursement are at the sole discretion of the lending institution. OctoWealth does not guarantee loan approval.

RBI Compliance

All loans are processed through RBI-registered banks and NBFCs. OctoWealth operates as a registered Direct Sales Agent (DSA). Interest rates are as per RBI guidelines and determined by the lending institution.
